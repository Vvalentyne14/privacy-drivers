Privacy Policy

Privacy and data Protection

Effective Date: October 16, 2024

1. Introduction

This policy outlines how Mavride handles the information collected from Drivers who use the app to provide transportation services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from Drivers:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details (email, phone), driver's license details, vehicle information, background check results, trip history, and payment details (for compensation).
  • Non-Personal Information: Device information, location data during trips, and app usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To assign and track transportation requests.
  • To ensure compliance with safety and legal requirements (e.g., background checks).
  • To process payments for trips completed.
  • To facilitate communication with Members.

4. Sharing Your Information

Your data may be shared with:

  • Members: Limited data such as your name and vehicle details will be shared for trip identification.
  • Brokers: To approve trip-related payments.
  • Providers: To help coordinate trips and manage services.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for the duration of your engagement as a Driver and as required for payment and legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

  • Access to your data.
  • Deletion of certain personal data where applicable.
  • Corrections or updates to your information.
